What we do

Critical Infrastructure Partners (CIP) is a private investment company focused on telecommunication infrastructure with an emphasis on middle and long-haul dark fiber optic routes throughout North America.

We partner with telecom providers and hyperscale users to provide best-in-class solutions to their clients and end users.
